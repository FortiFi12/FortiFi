Design Elements

The list of all the design elements alongside their justifications:

  • A colour scheme:
    • #FFFFFF "Clarity & Cleanliness" White represents safety, cleanliness, and professionalism. It’s the perfect backdrop to make information feel trustworthy and easy to read, crucial for a health-focused site.
    • #56918B "Health & Balance" This calming greenish-blue tone suggests wellness, stability, and recovery. It brings in a clinical yet approachable vibe, making users feel like they’re in good hands.
    • #E8872C "Energy & Action" Orange evokes movement, motivation, and performance. It grabs attention for calls-to-action while staying warm and positive.
    • #773AFD "Strength & Focus" Purple can symbolize discipline, focus, and elite performance, which are all important in sports. It also gives a modern, high-tech edge that aligns with performance tracking, coaching, or injury prevention tech.
    • #000000 "Authority, Precision & Boldness" Black conveys seriousness and professionalism. It adds contrast, makes content easier to read, and communicates that your site is credible and focused, exactly what users want when seeking guidance on injury prevention.
  • Font choices:
    • Racing Mark Race:
      It is a bold, high-impact display font that evokes speed, motion, and athletic intensity, which makes it perfect for headlines on a sports-focused site. Its strong and energetic appearance makes it ideal for capturing attention and reinforcing themes of competition, performance, and power, making it a natural fit for titles or branding on an injury prevention platform.
    • Quicksand:
      It is a modern, rounded sans-serif font that feels approachable, clean, and easy to read, making it a great choice for body text and informational content. Its friendly tone balances the boldness of Racing Mark Race, helping users feel comfortable while engaging with important health and safety information in a clear and accessible way.
  • User interface patterns:
    • Navigation menu - Created for accessing different sections of the Website.
    • Call to Action - Below our product a "Buy" button was added to help the customer take action quick and easy.
    • Hero Section - It consists of the logo, name and slogan with the intention of capturing the visitor's attention and to introduce our brand.
    • Scroll-down layout - This specific layout will ensure that the consumer will be fully introduced to our brand while scrolling through the platform.
    • Instagram/TikTok Button - They have the purpose of introducing our social media platforms to the visitors and to establish a loyal segment of customers.
  • The structure of the navigation and content (e.g. how content units are distributed across pages)
  • All these elements must be justified by referring to the theory and/or vocabulary of design
